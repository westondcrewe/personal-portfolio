Wang Globalnet

For over 45 years Wang Globalnet has been importing, manufacturing and distributing traditional Korean and Asian foods. Our first branch opened in Chicago in 1974, and since then we have established a vast network for food distribution to become the most trusted Asian food company.

We produce and distribute more than 2,000 items for retail and food service, in every category: dry grocery, chilled frozen foods, and also fresh produce. We are truly a One-Stop-Shop for all Korean and Asian food products. Food safety is our highest priority and we manufacture and distribute our products using only the highest quality ingredients under strict hygienic standards such as ISO, HACCP, GFSI.
